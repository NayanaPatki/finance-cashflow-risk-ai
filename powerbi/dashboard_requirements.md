# Dashboard Requirements

## Executive Liquidity

- KPI cards: current cash, 30-day net cash flow, runway, overdue AR, minimum forecast cash.
- 13-week ending-cash line with minimum-buffer reference line.
- Cash bridge from opening cash through inflows/outflows to ending cash.
- Exception queue for buffer breaches, overdue invoices, and anomalous expenses.

## Receivables

- Aging bands: current, 1-30, 31-60, 61-90, 90+.
- Customer concentration and overdue balance.
- Collection worklist with owner, action, and dispute status.

## Expense Risk

- Actual vs trailing category median.
- Exception table ranked by amount multiple and value.
- Supplier/category drillthrough.

## Scenario

- What-if parameters for revenue, expenses, collection delay, and one-time outflow.
- Ending cash, minimum cash, and breach week under selected assumptions.
- Base vs scenario comparison.

## Data Quality

- Refresh timestamp, record counts, missing keys, invalid categories, duplicate IDs, and model status.
